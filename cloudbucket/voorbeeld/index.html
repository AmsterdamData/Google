Big Query

Some data is available on Google Big Query, making it very easy and fast to query, filter and join the data. Take a look at: https://bigquery.cloud.google.com/table/amsterdam-smart-city-hackathon:traveltimes.traveltimes_jan2014

  • Travel times
    Travel times and velocity on 150+ routes in Amsterdam, both real-time and a historical archive of January 2014 (6.5 million data points)
  • Weather
    The weather (temperature, rainfall, wind, etc.) at Amsterdam for every 10 minutes of the last year.
  • Energy
    All building blocks (a group of houses, commercial space, etc.) in the City of Amsterdam, including geometry and all available data on energy consumption and potential.
  • Demographics
    Data on demographics of Amsterdam. For 286 main indicators, this table provides the most recent value in different geographic levels (buurten, stadsdelen, etc.). See this list of variables.
  • Fire department dispatches
    For all over 125.000 dispatches of Brandweer Amsterdam-Amstelland, the type of dispatch, location (at neighboorhood-level), date/time and priority are provided in this table.
  • Energy Prices 2014
    Data from PowerMatcherSuite on flexible energy prices, for each 15 minutes in 2014
